Books and Resources

I'll put my recommended here. Rememember to solve it accordingly.

  • Concept Of Physics 1,2 By H.C Verma: A great conceptual but rather easy book, but best for starters
  • Resnick Halliday( Idk which one as I have not solved this one personally but I hear it's a great book)
  • Problems In General Physics By I.E Irodov: is a great book and the collection of problems is great but not all problems are releveant so do your research before solving.
  • Pathfinder for Olympiad and JEE (Advanced) Physics: This book is peak, and I MEAN PEAKKKK. It is my favourite book and contains mind boggling and counter intuitive problems.This is a must buy for any physics enthusiast.
Of course the list doesn't end here but these are the only books I solve but I solve them honestly. There are many more books by authors such as David Morin.
